Register + Donate to Vision Walk 2023!

For 50 years, the Foundation Fighting Blindness has been the pioneer in innovative retinal disease research. Through gifts like yours, we have become a champion for the 10 million people — and millions more worldwide — affected by blinding retinal diseases. With the support of generous friends like you, we've been able to: Identify 270+ retinal disease-causing genes Launch more than 45 clinical trials for potential treatments Fund more than 82 research grants annually Together, we're winning! Donate to India Development and Relief Fund

India Development Relief Fund is a United States-based, 501(c)(3) tax-exempt public charity devoted to breaking the cycle of poverty in India, Nepal and Sri Lanka by empowering marginalized people with skills, services and infrastructure grounded in sustainable development. Their philosophy is powerful: "we believe a lasting change in impoverished regions can happen only when local people take charge and lead the change."
Computer Shiksha

Computer Shiksha’s strength as an organization lies in its ability to manage people, process and technology. In their dream to bridge the digital divide in India, they cater to all sections of society which are not computer literate. They start at the bottom of the pyramid by enabling those who have the least hope of getting computer literate in the near future.  Over 120k students and 650 schools/NGOs are benefiting from their program."
